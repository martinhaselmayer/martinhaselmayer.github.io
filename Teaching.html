Teaching

I’ve been teaching courses on Empirical data collection, Quantitative methods, Comparative Politics, Party competition, and the Austrian political system and the European Union for undergraduate students at the University of Vienna since 2014. Together with Wolfgang C. Müller, I have also taught a course on constitutional politics for Master students. At the University of Innsbruck, I have taught a seminar on party competition and political communication.

In the winter term, I'll give a workshop on crowdsourced text analysis at the Konstanz Methods Excellence Workshops (komex) with Alexander Horn. Information on my classes is available below.
