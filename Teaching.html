Teaching

Since 2014, I have been teaching undergraduate courses at the University of Vienna on topics such as Empirical Data Collection, Quantitative Methods, Comparative Politics, Party Competition, the Austrian Political System, and the European Union. Together with Wolfgang C. Müller, I have also co-taught a course on Constitutional Politics for Master's students.

In addition, I have taught a seminar on Party Competition and Political Communication at the University of Innsbruck and delivered a workshop on Crowdsourced Text Analysis at the Konstanz Methods Excellence Workshops (komex).
In the summer term of 2025, I will teach a seminar on Political communication of inequality" for BA students at the University of Vienna.
