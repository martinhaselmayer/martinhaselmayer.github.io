Welcome to my website!

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Applied Sciences (Hochschule Campus Wien) and the FORESIGHT Institute. In addition, I am a senior research fellow at the Department of Government (University of Vienna) and an associated researcher in the Varieties of Egalitarianism project at the University of Konstanz. Previously, I was a pre- and postdoctoral researcher in the Austrian National Election Study (AUTNES) and a postdoctoral researcher at the Cluster of Excellence: The Politics of Inequality (University of Konstanz).

My research has appeared in journals such as the American Political Science Review , the British Journal of Political Science , the European Journal of Political Research , and Perspectives on Politics .

My work has also been covered in national and international media, including Fortune, Radio Ö1, Der Standard, Frankfurter Allgemeine Zeitung, and Mediapart.

This website provides information on my research and teaching.
