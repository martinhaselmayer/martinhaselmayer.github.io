Welcome to my website!

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the FORESIGHT Institute, a senior research fellow at the Department of Government (University of Vienna), and an associated researcher at the Varieties of Egalitarianism project at the University of Konstanz. Previously, I was a pre- and postdoctoral researcher in the Austrian National Election Study (AUTNES) and a postdoctoral researcher at the Cluster of Excellence: The Politics of Inequality (University of Konstanz). This website provides information on my research and teaching.
